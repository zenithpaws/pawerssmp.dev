Server Plugin Commands

DiscordSRV

/discord link | Sends you instructions to link your Minecraft account with your Discord account

/discord linked | Shows if your Minecraft account is linked with a discord account

/discord unlink | Unlink your Discord account from your Minecraft account


Dynmap

/dynmap hide | Hides the player from the map

/dynmap show | Shows the player on the map

/dynmap url | Show the URL for the map


EzHomes

/home <home> | Teleport to one of your homes

/homes | See your list of homes

/sethome <home> | Set a new home

/delhome <home> | Delete one of your homes

/respawnhome <home> | Set a new home and spawnpoint

/back | Teleport where you last teleported from


GSit

/sit (/gsit) | Sit on a block

/lay (/glay) | Lay on a block

/bellyflop (/gbellyflop) | Bellyflop on a block

/spin (/gspin) | Spin on a block

/crawl (/gcrawl) | Crawl on the ground

/sit toggle/playertoggle | Toggle the ability to click blocks or players


GWarp

/warp <warpname> (Current warps are: Astral, Nether, Stronghold, End)


Simple TPA

/tpa <player> | Ask to teleport to another player

/tpahere <player> | Ask another player to teleport to you

/tpaccept (/tpyes) | Accept a teleport request

/tpdeny | Deny a teleport request


Simple RTP

/rtp | Teleport to a random location
